Classic Chocolate Cake

Description:

Indulge in the rich and decadent taste of Classic Chocolate Cake. This timeless dessert is a perfect blend of moist chocolate layers and velvety chocolate frosting. Baked to perfection, the cake is a true delight for chocolate enthusiasts. Each bite is a heavenly experience, making it an ideal treat for special occasions or a delightful ending to any meal.

    Ingredients:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water
    Steps:
  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. Step 2: In a large bowl, sift together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Step 3: Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until well combined.
  4. Step 4: Stir in boiling water until the batter is smooth. The batter will be thin, but that's okay!
  5. Step 5: Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
  6. Step 6: Bake for 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Step 7: Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
  8. Step 8: Frost the cooled cakes with your favorite chocolate frosting and enjoy!
